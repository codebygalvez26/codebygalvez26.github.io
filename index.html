From Cupang Antipolo City

When I reached the twelfth grade, I thought it would be just as easy as the eleventh grade, but I was wrong. For me it was too hard, because of the grading system. If you don't pass any activity, you don't get a grade. I don't want to repeat or experience summer class, and I want to be able to march with my fellow students, and because of that I have to study hard.
